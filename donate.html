Frequently Asked Questions

How much of my donation goes directly to programs? expand_more

We pride ourselves on radical transparency. 85% of all donations go directly towards our on-the-ground programs, including kit distribution and educational workshops. The remaining 15% sustains our operational infrastructure, allowing us to reach more communities efficiently.

Are my donations tax-deductible? expand_more

Yes. For The Menstruator is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. You will receive an automated receipt via email immediately after your contribution, which can be used for tax purposes.

Can I donate physical products instead of money? expand_more

Absolutely. We accept un-opened, factory-sealed menstrual products at our regional drop-off locations. Please visit our "Get Involved" page to find a physical donation center near you.
