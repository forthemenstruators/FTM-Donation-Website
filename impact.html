Why This Matters

Lack of access to menstrual products and education creates significant barriers. It leads to missed school days, health complications, and a deep sense of shame.

By addressing menstrual health, we are not just providing products; we are empowering individuals, fostering dignity, and breaking down systemic inequalities.

"FTM changed how I see myself. I no longer feel ashamed."
