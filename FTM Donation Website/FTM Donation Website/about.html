OUR STORY

Empowering Dignity,
Period.

For The Menstruator (FTM) is a grassroots, student-led organization dedicated to eradicating period poverty. We believe that access to menstrual products is a fundamental human right, not a luxury.

The Silent Crisis

Period poverty affects millions, hindering education, work, and basic human dignity. It's a systemic issue that demands compassionate action.

1 in 4

Teens miss class due to lack of access to menstrual products.

500 Million

People worldwide lack adequate facilities for menstrual hygiene management. This staggering number represents lost opportunities, compromised health, and diminished confidence on a global scale.

Financial Burden

The "Tampon Tax" disproportionately affects low-income individuals.
