Common Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

expand_more

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. During high-volume periods, it may take slightly longer, but your message is important to us.

Can I volunteer remotely?

expand_more

Yes! We have numerous digital advocacy, content creation, and administrative roles that can be done from anywhere in the world.

Where do my donations go?

expand_more

Donations directly fund our grassroots distribution of menstrual products and our educational programs. We maintain full transparency in our annual impact reports.
